Leave Request Guidelines
Approval Criteria
  • Medical Leaves: Verify that supporting documentation is mentioned or will be provided.
  • Personal Leaves: Evaluate the importance and necessity of the request.
  • Academic Events: Verify the event details and relevance to the student's coursework.
  • Religious Observances: These should generally be approved as part of diversity and inclusion policies.
Effect of Approval
  • Approved leave requests mark the student as "present" for all sessions during the leave period.
  • These attendances are marked with a note indicating they were due to approved leave.
  • Approved absences do not count against the student's required attendance percentage.
  • Students are still responsible for any coursework missed during their leave period.
When a leave request is approved, attendance is automatically marked for all sessions during the leave period. This can be overridden manually if needed.
